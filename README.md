## Suolingo - A Science-Learning App for Middle School Students
Suolingo is a gamified science-learning web app I adapted for my tutoring student, built on top of an open-source [Duolingo](https://www.duolingo.com) clone. I redesigned it around science content, creating custom characters and writing original problems tailored to my students' learning needs.

[suolingo-science.vercel.app
](https://suolingo-science.vercel.app)

## Educational Intent
This project applies the mechanics that make Duolingo effective — streaks, bite-sized lessons, immediate feedback, and playful characters — to one-on-one science tutoring. Beyond simply using the app, my student took part in making it: she drew the characters that appear throughout Suolingo, becoming co-creator of their own learning tool. The overarching goal was to increase engagement, in two ways - by making practice between tutoring sessions feel like a game rather than homework, and by giving the student a sense of ownership over the app by involving her in its design.
